 ### What is BRC-20 and Ordinals？ 

BRC-20 is an experimental token standard on the Bitcoin blockchain modeled on Ethereum’s ERC-20.  

The Ordinals protocol, which was officially launched on December 14, 2022, utilizes the technical features of SegWit and Taproot3 on the Bitcoin network to enable the direct minting, transfer and burn of NFT on the Bitcoin.
